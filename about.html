How We Started

Our founder, Natalie Bourdakos, started this club with the hopes of sprinkling smiles with every sundae! Inspiration for club came from her own hospital stay as a child when a pint of cookies and cream ice cream was her only source of smiles. With the help of her roommate, Catarina Marchioli, they were able to turn this vision into reality!

Our mission at the Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh

Our main mission is to sprinkle smiles! Every weekend, our volunteers go to the Children's Hospital of Pittsburgh, and serve ice cream and toppings to the patients and their families. We hope to sweeten the day of every person we meet!

Make-A-Wish

As an extra project, we have started raising money to help fund a child's wish! We hope to make this an annual project, and truly sprinkle smiles to all the kid's who need it.
